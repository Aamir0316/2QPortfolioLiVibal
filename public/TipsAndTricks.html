VALORANT

Pro Tips and Tricks for The Controller

1. Be confident in entering the site first!

Don't wait for your teammates to start getting mad at you. Make sure you are brave and confident in you abilities and make a leeway for ur team to go in.

2. Don't bait your teammates.

The last thing you want to do as a duelist is bait your teammates. It's dishonorful to choose an agent and not follow it's roles. Instead go in first, clear corners and always stay in front!

3. Communicate and plan with your team

As a duelist, you're job is to enter site first. However, without the help of your team, this is almost impossible. Communicate with your team and tell them which angles you'll be clearing or direction you're entering.

Pros and Cons of using a duelist

Pros Cons How To Counter These Cons
You gain courage to enter site for your team. You risk dying at the start of the round if you enter wrong. Enter site first but still be careful and slow down when clearing corners.
Your abilities challenge the enemies and gives you an advantage. In most cases, you might also affect your teammates through your abilities. Watch out for your teammates as they might get hit. Make sure no one's in the way.
You'll most of the time get a higher kill death ratio than your teammates. Most duelists rely on aim but some people struggle with that. If you use your abilities to it's maximum potential, aim would only take about 40% of the impact.
Most of the duelists' abilities are made for the person using them. Teammates might complain that you're being greedy or selfish. If you know you're in the right, just mute them. However feedback should always be taken.
If a round ends well because of your job, the enemies may get tilted or scared giving you an advantage. Too much ego from scaring people may result in overconfidence. Know your limit and capabilities.
