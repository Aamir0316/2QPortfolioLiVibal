VALORANT

An Overview Of: The Duelist

Duelists are one of the four roles that agents are divided into in VALORANT.

The abilities of agents under this role allow them to give way for their teammates by entering the site with pressure first. They make sure to clear every angle that the enemy may be.

They play a big role in overall fighting and taking control of the site for their teammates to safely go in.

As of October 2025, the game currently has 8 duelist agents in its roster.
